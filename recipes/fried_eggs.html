Fried Eggs Recipe

Back to Home

Description:

This is a simple and classic fried eggs recipe, perfect for a quick and satisfying meal. I often eat this for breakfast or brunch because it is easy to make and delicious.

Ingredients:

  1. 2 eggs
  2. Salt and pepper to taste
  3. Olive oil for frying

Steps:

  1. Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat and add olive oil.
  2. Crack the eggs into the skillet and cook until the whites are set and the yolks reach your desired level of doneness.
  3. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Serve hot and enjoy!
