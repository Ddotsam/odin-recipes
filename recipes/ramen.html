Ramen Recipe

Back to Home

Description:

This is a simple and delicious bowl of ramen noodle soup, perfect for a quick and satisfying meal. I often eat this for lunch when I am pressed for time because it tastes great and is very easy to make. The only qualms I have is that it is not highly nutritious.

Ingredients:

  1. 1 package of instant ramen noodles
  2. 3 cups of water
  3. 1 egg (optional)
  4. 1 green onion, chopped (optional)
  5. Soy sauce or miso paste (optional)

Steps:

  1. Boil the water in a pot.
  2. Add the ramen noodles and cook according to the package instructions.
  3. Remove from the heat.
  4. If desired, add the egg and cook until it reaches your preferred level of doneness.
  5. Stir in the soy sauce or miso paste, if using.
  6. Garnish with chopped green onions, if desired.
  7. Serve hot and enjoy!
