Sandwich Recipe

Back to Home

Description:

This is a classic take on a ham and cheese sandwich, perfect for a quick and satisfying meal. I eat it for lunch nearly every day, minus the cheese because it hurts my tummy.

Ingredients:

  1. 2 slices of bread
  2. 1 slice of ham
  3. 1 slice of cheese (optional)
  4. Butter or mayonnaise
  5. Lettuce and tomato (optional)
