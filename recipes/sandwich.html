Sandwich Recipe

Back to Home

Description:

This is a classic take on a ham and cheese sandwich, perfect for a quick and satisfying meal. I eat it for lunch nearly every day, minus the cheese because it hurts my tummy.

Ingredients:

  1. 2 slices of bread
  2. 1 slice of ham
  3. 1 slice of cheese (optional)
  4. Butter or mayonnaise (NOT optional)
  5. Lettuce and tomato (optional)

Steps:

  1. Spread butter or mayonnaise on one side of each slice of bread.
  2. Place the ham and cheese (if using) on one slice of bread.
  3. Add lettuce and tomato (if using) on top of the ham and cheese.
  4. Top with the other slice of bread, butter or mayonnaise side down.
  5. Cut the sandwich in half and serve.
